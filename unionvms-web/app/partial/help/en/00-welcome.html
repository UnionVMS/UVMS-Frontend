Welcome to Union VMS

What is Union VMS?

Union VMS provides you with real-time information on ship movements and ports. It allows you to track and control all active assets through live position reporting.

Union VMS also allows you to view, export and print reports to secure that responsible and legitimate fishing are performed.

Who should use Union VMS?

It is intended that the one-site person who is responsible for the asset tracking and reporting system, is to be responsible for maintaining the Union VMS.

However, it can be used by several users with different accounts.
